01 — Bedroom

Your Sanctuary.
Perfectly Designed.

A bedroom is more than a room to sleep in. It's the first space you see every morning and the last at night. We design it to feel like a luxury retreat — private, restful, and uniquely yours.

Hydraulic Bed

Gas-lift hydraulic storage beds that open smoothly to reveal a full under-mattress storage compartment. Premium upholstered headboard included.

Drawer Bed

Beds with built-in pull-out drawers on the sides — perfect for linen, seasonal clothing, and extra storage without compromising the bedroom aesthetic.

Wall Mounted Bed

Murphy-style wall-mounted folding beds — ideal for studio apartments and guest rooms. Folds away cleanly to free up the full floor space when not in use.

Foam Headboard

Custom upholstered foam headboards in premium fabric, velvet, or leatherette. Wall-fixed or bed-integrated, designed to anchor your bedroom's visual identity.

Wardrobes — Full System

Floor-to-ceiling wardrobes with: Drawers, Cloth Hanging, Built-in Dressing Mirror, Organisers, Iron Board, and dedicated compartments for every need. Soft-close Hettich fittings throughout.

Side Tables

Custom bedside tables designed to match your bed and wardrobe — with integrated charging points, drawers, or open shelving. Any size, any finish.

Study Table

Wall-mounted or free-standing study tables with overhead shelves, cable management, and a dedicated workspace for children and home-office needs.

Book Rack

Open or closed book racks — wall-mounted floating shelves, built-in alcove shelving, or freestanding units. Designed to display your collection beautifully.

Loft Storage

Overhead loft cabinets above wardrobes for seasonal storage. Smooth-action lift-up or hinged doors — maximizing every vertical inch of your bedroom.

Wall Décor

Feature walls with paneling, fluted panels, back-painted glass, wallpapers, or textured finishes — transforming a plain wall into your bedroom's signature statement.

06 — Lighting Design

Light Changes
Everything.

The right lighting makes every material, texture, and colour in your home look its best. We design lighting as an integral part of the interior — not an afterthought.

Panel Lights

Surface-mounted panel lights and rectangular LED panels for kitchens, offices, and utility spaces — uniform, glare-free illumination in warm or cool white.

Profile Lights

Aluminum LED profile channels recessed into furniture edges, under cabinets, and ceiling coving. Creates a luxury glow that makes your kitchen and wardrobes look premium.

Magnetic Lights

Modern magnetic track systems — repositionable spotlights that click into a ceiling-mounted magnetic rail. Change your lighting layout any time without any electrical work.

Spot Lights

Recessed downlights precisely aimed at artwork, décor items, and kitchen platforms — creating drama and depth with targeted illumination where it matters most.

Track Lights

Adjustable track lighting mounted to ceiling rails — ideal for kitchens, art walls, and retail-style living rooms. Aims precisely where you need it.

Cove Lights

Indirect LED strips hidden inside false ceiling coves — washing the ceiling and walls with a warm ambient glow. The defining feature of any luxury living room.
