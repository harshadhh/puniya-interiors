Our Craft

Built in Pune.
Made to Last.

Our workshop is right here in Pune. When you choose Puniya Interiors, you are choosing a piece made by skilled local craftsmen — not factory-flat-packed and assembled on site.

We use only BWP marine ply for all carcasses, ensuring your furniture survives Pune's humid monsoons for decades without swelling or warping. Finishes are UV-sealed for lasting colour fidelity.

  • BWP Marine Ply — Moisture & termite resistant
