Frequently Asked

Questions About
Our Services

How long does a modular kitchen take in Pune?

Puniya Interiors delivers complete modular kitchens in Pune within 45 days of design approval — including 3D design, fabrication, site installation, and final finishing. We handle everything in-house, which is why we can guarantee the timeline.

Do you offer a warranty on furniture?

Yes. Every piece of furniture manufactured by Puniya Interiors comes with a 10-year warranty covering material defects, structural issues, and hardware performance. We use only BWP Marine Ply and European Hettich & Häfele fittings throughout.

Which hardware brands do you use?

We exclusively use European hardware — Hettich (Germany) and Häfele (Germany) — for all kitchen and furniture fittings. This includes soft-close drawer runners, hinges, magic corners, tandem pull-outs, and lift systems.

Do you do civil work and renovation in Pune?

Yes. We are a true one-stop solution. Our in-house team handles false ceilings, painting, plumbing, electrical, tiling, and complete home renovation across Pune — so you never need to coordinate with multiple contractors.

Which areas of Pune do you serve?

We serve all major areas of Pune: Dhanori, Viman Nagar, Kharadi, Wakad, Baner, Aundh, Koregaon Park, Hadapsar, NIBM Road, Hinjewadi and more. Our showroom is in Dhanori, Pune — visit us Monday to Saturday, 10 AM – 7 PM.

Is the site visit and consultation free?

Yes, completely free. We visit your home, take measurements, discuss your design preferences and budget — with zero commitment. We also provide an initial 3D concept within 5 working days of the site visit at no charge.
