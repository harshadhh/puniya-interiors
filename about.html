Our Story

Pune's One-Stop
Interior Partner

Puniya Interiors started in Dhanori, Pune in 2015 with one belief: that every family deserves a home designed with the same care, quality, and precision as the premium projects they see in magazines — at a price that is honest, transparent, and fair.

We started with a single workshop and a promise: never compromise on materials. Today, that promise has taken us into 200+ homes across Pune — from modular kitchens and wardrobes to false ceilings, renovation, lighting, wallpapers, and commercial fit-outs. One team. Everything handled.

What makes us different is not just the quality of what we build. It is the commitment to delivering it in 45 days, backed by a 10-year furniture warranty — and being there long after handover if anything ever needs attention.
