Our Story

A Pune Studio
Built on Honesty

Puniya Interiors started in 2015 from a simple belief: that every family in Pune deserves a home designed with the same care and quality as the premium projects they saw in magazines — at a price that was honest and transparent.

We started with a single workshop in Dhanori and a promise to never compromise on materials. Today, that same promise has taken us across Pune — from Viman Nagar to Baner, from Kharadi to Wakad — and into over 200 families' homes.

Every project is personal. We remember the families. We remember the challenges. And we are proudest not of the awards, but of the clients who come back to us when they move to a new home.
